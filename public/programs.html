Peer Coaching Circles

Our coaching circles are monthly themed group sessions built around guided conversation, shared experience, and intentional growth. Each session includes curated discussion prompts, handouts, and a take-home journal prompt card.

Guided conversations rooted in shared experience. Every participant leaves with a journal prompt card and a few things to try at home.
