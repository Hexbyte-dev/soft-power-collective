Work With Casey

Every engagement is named for the transformation it delivers.

Every engagement here has a defined scope, a threshold to cross, and a point where you no longer need the framework held for you because you're holding it yourself. That's the goal, and we name it on day one.

Is This You?

This work is for you if…

You have the spark of the idea (the business, the promotion, the next chapter) and you're stuck in the action phase. You're disciplined; you don't need to be told to work harder. You want someone to hold the framework steady while you cross, and you're ready to supply the motion.

It isn't, if…

You're looking for someone to do the work for you, a cheerleader who never pushes back, or a coach who bills hours indefinitely. Expectations here are high because I'm certain you can meet them. If that sentence makes you sit up straighter, keep reading.

The Engagements

Three ways to cross, depending on the size of the threshold in front of you. Investment is discussed on the discovery call, once we both know the engagement fits.

The Crossing

3 months · 6 sessions

For a single, well-defined threshold: the ask you've been putting off, the role you're ready to claim, the first paying client. Six focused sessions with between-session support to keep the motion going.

The Threshold Intensive

6 months · 12 sessions

The core engagement: idea to operating reality. Twelve sessions, email and voice support between them, and a written progress review at the midpoint so we can both see the crossing happening in evidence rather than in feelings.

The Held Framework

12 months · ongoing partnership

For builders with more than one threshold ahead. A year of sessions, priority access, and quarterly deep-dive reviews. Some people don't need to be developed; they need to be believed at, structurally. This is that container.

Not Ready for a Full Engagement?

The Deep-Dive Diagnostic

A single 90-minute session built around one question: what is actually keeping you stuck? You leave with a written pattern summary (the honest diagnosis most people have never been given) and a clear next move, whether or not we ever work together again.
