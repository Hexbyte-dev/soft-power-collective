About Me

Hi, I’m Casey. A single mom, lifelong rebrander, career woman, and cycle-breaker on a mission.

I grew up between extremes—an authoritarian parent on one side, emotional distance on the other. I learned early to read the room before I learned to feel my own emotions. Later, I became a single mom, navigating the heartbreak of addiction, codependency, and the quiet ache of never having had a true model for safety or intimacy.

But something shifted.

I began to rebuild. Not just a home for my child, but a home within myself. Through trauma-informed healing, nervous system education, and embodied exploration, I discovered a deeper truth: the body holds wisdom that words often can’t reach. And in the right spaces, intimacy becomes not just sensual, but transformational.

It was in those spaces, through conscious kink, aftercare, and emotionally intelligent connection, that I began reclaiming my voice. My power. My softness. And I realized: healing isn’t about getting it right, it’s about showing up whole, even when we’re still in pieces.

The Soft Power Collective was born from that knowing.
This isn’t about performance. It’s about presence.
It’s about creating space for others to feel seen, safe, curious, and connected, whether you’re exploring kink, opening your relationship, or just learning how to show up with more honesty and care.

You don’t have to be fearless to begin.
You just have to be willing to come home to yourself.
