You Belong Here

Whether you're exploring embodiment, navigating an open relationship, or simply looking for a space to explore who you are with honesty and care — you belong here. The Soft Power Collective is the community around Casey Lynn's intimacy coaching practice, rooted in emotional intelligence, consent, and the belief that vulnerability is a form of strength.

What We Offer

Peer Coaching Circles

Themed group sessions exploring embodiment, relationships, and self-discovery through guided conversation.

Mentor Pairings

Workshops + Events

Upcoming events and workshops from the collective.

Book Club & Resources

Meet Casey Lynn

Casey is an intimacy coach. She works with curious people — singles, partners, couples — on consent, embodiment, non-monogamy, and the quieter work of coming home to yourself. The Soft Power Collective is the community she built around her practice, so the learning doesn't have to happen alone.
