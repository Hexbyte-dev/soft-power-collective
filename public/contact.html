Frequently Asked Questions

Who is threshold coaching for?

People with the want and the spark who are stuck in the action phase: emerging leaders ready for a seat that doesn't exist yet, builders with an idea that hasn't become an operating reality, and career-changers standing between chapters. You don't need a title to belong here, just a threshold.

What does working with Casey look like?

Working with Casey is a defined-scope engagement rather than open-ended sessions. We name the threshold on day one, agree on what crossing it looks like, and then Casey holds the framework (structure, external belief, and honest pushback) while you supply the motion. Sessions mix diagnosis, skill-building, and the kind of high expectations that come from certainty. Details of pacing and scope are worked out on the discovery call.

Is everything confidential?

Absolutely. What's shared in coaching stays there. Case studies on this site are anonymized and shared only with permission. Trust is the bedrock of the work, and it's treated that way.

Does Casey have coaching certifications?

The framework is built on a documented decade of doing the work: eight written case studies, six of eight direct reports promoted, and coaching relationships that kept producing results years after the reporting lines ended. Continued study in developmental psychology and coaching craft is ongoing. If a specific credential matters to your context, ask on the discovery call and you'll get a straight answer.

How much does it cost?

Investment depends on the engagement, and it's discussed openly on the discovery call, once we both know whether the fit is right. The call itself is free, and it's about finding the right fit rather than making a pitch. There's no pressure either way.
