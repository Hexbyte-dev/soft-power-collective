Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this community for?

Anyone exploring intimacy, power dynamics, embodiment, or relationships — whether you've been in these spaces for years or you're just starting to ask questions. Curiosity is enough. You don't need a label to belong here.

What does working with Casey look like?

Individual coaching with Casey is a confidential, one-on-one coaching relationship focused on your intimate, relational, and emotional life. The work draws on trauma-informed practice, nervous system education, and embodied study. Sessions are a mix of honest conversation, skill-building, and holding space for what comes up between. Whether you're untangling a relationship, exploring embodiment or non-monogamy for the first time, or looking for a grounded thinking partner, coaching meets you where you are. Specifics — session length, pacing, and what a package looks like — are worked out in your first conversation.

Is everything confidential?

Absolutely. Privacy and discretion are foundational to everything we do. What's shared in our circles, mentorship sessions, and events stays there. We take this seriously because trust is the bedrock of the work we do together.

I'm brand new to all of this. Will I feel out of place?

This space was built for you. Whether you're just asking the first question or you've been at this a while, you'll find a welcoming community here. We don't expect anyone to have it all figured out — that's the whole point. Come as you are.

How much does it cost?

Pricing fits where you are — Casey's individual coaching, peer circles, and mentor pairings are priced differently, and we'll walk through what makes sense. Reach out through the form above or email us directly. An honest first conversation. No pressure either way.
