Book Club

What Is the Book Club?

Each month, we choose a book that speaks to the themes at the heart of our community — emotional intelligence, embodiment, non-monogamy, and honest relationships. We read together, then come together for guided discussion.

Whether you join us virtually or in person, the book club is open to all experience levels. No background required — just curiosity and a willingness to reflect.
